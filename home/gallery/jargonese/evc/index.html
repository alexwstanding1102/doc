> comparison between Blender rendering engines Eevee and Cycles!!

> hello!! this post is mostly gonna just be comparisons between results in eevee and cycles, with some details and explanations of which one's better at doing certain things.


> while cycles yields objectively better results, with more features like anistropic materials, ray traced light sources, actual reflections of other objects in the scene, etc., eevee has the upper hand in one sector of the rendering game: speed. eevee is quite a lot faster than cycles, which of course makes it a considerable choice for those who do not have the time and power to render out thousands of frames in a rather quick time frame. that is the objective fact, however I personally go for cycles, no matter how long it takes; mostly because having such good lighting makes it worth it instead of cutting a couple of days out of the process. denoising tools exist, and they absolutely get the job of cutting down time done. cycles is my go to.
